Water Quality Lab Analysis

According to JJM Guidelines, each lab is required to test at least 3000 samples per financial year

RDW&SD Water Quality Testing Lab Yadgir is performing good as it has surpassed 3000 sample test threshold value

RDW&SD Water Quality Testing Lab Shorapur has a little bit decreased performance as of Yadgir lab

Different Water Quality Labs and samples tested by them in FY 2023-24

Water Testing Analysis:

  • A total of 5996 water samples were tested in Yadgir District across two labs.
  • RDW&SD stands for RURAL DRINKING WATER AND SANITATION DEPARTMENT
  • The RDW&SD Water Quality Testing Lab Yadgir tested the majority of the samples at 3272. The Shorapur lab tested the remaining 2724 samples.
  • This indicates the district-level Yadgir lab has greater testing capacity than the smaller block-level Shorapur lab.
  • However, some villages located closer to the one lab were sending samples to farther labs. A cost analysis is recommended to understand why villages are bypassing their nearest lab.
  • Optimizing sample flows to the lowest cost labs could increase savings in the testing budget.

Additional data on quarterly and village-level figures could reveal more trends to inform decisions. Expanding budget for labs needing equipment upgrades may also help improve capabilities.
