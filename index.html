Overview of Yadgir, Karnataka

Yadgir district is located in the state of Karnataka, India. It has a population of about 1.15 million based on the 2011 census data.

Key Facts

  • It has 6 administrative blocks - Hunsagi, Shorapur, Shahapur, Shahpur, Yadgir and Wadgera
  • The district spans over about 195,360 hectares of land
  • It falls under the Krishna Basin region and receives an average annual rainfall of about 600 mm
  • The main rivers are the Krishna and the Bhima
  • Agriculture and allied activities are the main occupation, with major crops being cotton, wheat and millets

Jal Jeevan Mission Goals

  • Provide tap connections to all rural households for regular, long-term supply of safe drinking water
  • Ensure water quality and reduce water-borne diseases
  • Empower communities in planning, implementing, operating & maintaining systems
  • Promote groundwater recharge, conservation and reuse

Key Achievements

  • Increased tap water coverage from 3.23 Cr to 13.98 Cr households
  • 9-fold increase in connections in backward districts
  • 23.55 lakh women trained for water quality testing
  • 206 partner organizations and 99 institutions engaged
